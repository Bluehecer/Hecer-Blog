About ScamShield

The ScamShield App is a powerful AI-powered protection system that detects scammers in real-time, blocks fraud, and even alerts authorities nearby. It’s designed to provide digital safety for everyone, protecting your calls, messages, and links.

Features

  • Real-time scam detection
  • Automatic blocking of fraud attempts
  • Alerts local authorities for serious threats
  • User-friendly interface
